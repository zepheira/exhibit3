Exhibit 3.0 Scripted Demo: UN Millennium Development Goal 4

Millennium Development Goal Target 4.A: Reduce by two thirds, between 1990 and 2015, the under-five mortality rate.

As of 2011, this is the status of achieving MDG4A by country. Countries that have cut their rate by the goal already are meets or exceeds (over 75%); countries above a 50% cut are close; countries above a 33% cut are half way to the goal; countries below that are poor, and some have gotten worse. Developed countries are not included in regional or other groupings, only in the Developed grouping; regions may overlap (Landlocked and Least Developed share a number of members). Data source.
